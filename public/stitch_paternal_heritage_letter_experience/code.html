To My Father, Arthur

A tribute to the man who shaped my world through strength, wisdom, and quiet love.

expand_more

"They say that greatness is not found in possessions, but in the character of a man. As I look back through the years, I see your greatness in every sacrifice you made."

I remember the early mornings, the sound of your car leaving the driveway before the sun had even touched the horizon. You never complained about the long hours or the weight of responsibility. To you, it was simply what a father did—building a foundation for us to stand upon.

But beyond the work, it was the quiet moments that defined you in my eyes. The way you listened without interrupting, the firm grip of your hand on my shoulder when I felt lost, and the subtle wink you'd give me across the dinner table. Those were the lessons in empathy and resilience that I carry with me today.

"You taught me that strength isn't loud. It's the steady hand in the storm."

JUNE 16, 2024

Dear Dad,

I wrote this to tell you things I don't say often enough. Thank you for being my North Star. Thank you for the integrity that guided our family, and for the laughter that filled our home even in the leanest times.

You are my hero, not for one single act, but for the thousand small ones you performed every day of my life.

With all my love and deepest respect,

SIGNED BY

Your Son, James

LOCATION

New York City
